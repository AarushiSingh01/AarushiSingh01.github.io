SISSuccess In Schools

About

SIS allows high school teachers to put their student data in, such as tests and labs, and it graphically shows the the growth or decline of students overtime.

For example...

If a teacher puts in her students overall grades from the first semester, she may see that her students are doing well and improving in her class. However, when she puts in her student's test grades, she sees the opposite. Her students have not improved on tests, and the reason why she couldn't see this problem earlier was because the other grades were covering it up. The teacher can now clearly see what topics her students struggled in and now she is aware that she must do something different to help her students improve on the tests. SIS is the tool to help students succeed in school!
